Services

_____

We offer our clients an array of unique value-added services aimed at improving patient outcomes while reducing the overall cost of care. These services can benefit all healthcare stakeholders, including health insurers, Accountable Care Organizations (ACO’s), Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBM’s), physicians, and patients.

NextGen Precision™ Testing

Our NextGen Precision Testing technology can detect drug concentrations at significantly lower levels than the industry standard. With the elimination of false negatives, you can identify and aid patients who need the most assistance to improve their outcomes.

Learn More
Precision Analytics

A customizable dashboard to display data in an infinite number of ways to help manage patient drug testing information, including prescription adherence metrics, test utilization history, specific drug information, risk stratification, and tracking patient trends and outcomes.

Learn More
Precision TBM

Toxicology Benefit Management (TBM) is a system that brings together pharmacy benefit managers, insurers, pharmacies, providers, and patients for more transparent and effective prescription adherence monitoring.

Learn More
Precision DDI

A customizable dashboard to display metrics for patient drug-drug interactions, including DDI level classification, DDI drug contribution metrics, and drugs not ordered resulting in missed DDIs.
